City Omelette

    Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Goat Cheese

Want an easy omelette that isn't boring?

This recipe brings together the countryside and urban gastronomy in a fast and easy fashion.

Recipe

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Apply butter to one side evenly, wait for it to melt
  3. Apply the jelly
  4. On the other side, apply the peanut butter
  5. Finish with a zig-zag pattern of honey
