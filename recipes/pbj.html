The Traditional Peanut Butter & Jelly

    Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • Organic Peanut Butter
  • Fig Jelly
  • Honey
  • Butter

Why Spend the time on recreating the easiest sandwich in the world?

This recipe restores the peanut butter and jelly sandwich back to it's roots with care and taste! The American peanut butter and jelly sandwich has lost it's way in recent years, due to processed foods dominating our shelves and American culture paying less and less attention to the way they eat. Here's how to restore it.

Recipe

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Apply butter to one side evenly, wait for it to melt
  3. Apply the jelly
  4. On the other side, apply the peanut butter
  5. Finish with a zig-zag pattern of honey
